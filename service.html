Special Services For Terra Zo Visitors

Car Parking
Make your visit to the zoo even more enjoyable with our convenient and easily accessible car parking facilities.
Animal Photos
Bring the zoo home with our captivating collection of animal photos! From majestic lions to playful penguins, discover incredible diversity and capture the essence of wildlife in every click.
Guide Services
Get closer to the animals and delve deeper into their world with our personalized guided tours. Our knowledgeable guides will tailor the experience to your interests and answer all your questions.
Food & Beverages
Make the most of your family day at the zoo with our convenient and delicious food options! Pack a picnic basket with pre-ordered treats or enjoy our family-friendly restaurants featuring kid-approved favorites and comfortable seating for everyone.
Zoo Shopping
Take home a piece of the zoo with our delightful selection of souvenirs! Find unique gifts, adorable animal-themed toys, and charming keepsakes that will remind you of your unforgettable adventure.
Rest House
Take a break from your zoo adventure and unwind in our tranquil Rest House. Whether you need to feed your little one, change a diaper, or simply enjoy a moment of peace, our comfortable haven offers a welcome respite. Relax in our spacious seating areas, enjoy convenient amenities like restrooms and changing tables, and recharge for your next exploration.
